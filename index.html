The Artist

ISAM is a pianist, composer, and live electronic performer whose music places the piano at the center of a powerful Afro-Electro universe.

Blending synthesizers, keyboards, and live vocals, his performances unfold as a musical journey moving fluidly from classical influences and Western electronic rhythms to upbeat dance grooves infused with oriental melodies.

Driven by improvisation and live interaction, ISAM’s shows are immersive experiences where acoustic and electronic worlds collide. His music reflects movement, travel, and cultural dialogue — connecting ancestral rhythms with contemporary club energy.

TUBES EP - JUNE 2025

TUBES EP is a five-track release developed from ISAM's live performances. The EP translates the energy and dynamics of the stage into recorded form, combining piano, synthesizers, electronic rhythms, and vocals.

Rooted in improvisation, TUBES reflects ISAM's signature Afro-electronic sound, bridging live musicianship with contemporary dance and melodic influences.
