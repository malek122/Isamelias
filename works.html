Selected Works & Collaborations

A showcase of musical projects and bands featuring Isam Elias.

Ghazall Band

Founded in Nazareth in 2015, Ghazall was a five-member band that played a significant role in the local alternative scene. Performing original material ranging from soft rock to independent reggae influences, the band released one full-length album and several singles. As pianist, keyboardist, vocalist, and co-writer, formed the backbone of the band’s sound and musical direction.

Zenobia

Founded in 2017, Zenobia is an electro duo blending synthesizers, driving rhythms, and contemporary electronic production. The project released the EP KSR KSR KSR (2019), followed by the full-length album Halak Halak (2020), and the EP Warriors Never Die (2023). As one half of the duo, Isam was involved in composition, production, and live performance.
